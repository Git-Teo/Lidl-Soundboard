boolean org.schabi.newpipe.player.playback.PlaybackListener.isApproachingPlaybackEdge ( final long  timeToEndMillis)

Called to check if the currently playing stream is approaching the end of its playback. Implementation should return true when the current playback position is progressing within timeToEndMillis or less to its playback during.

May be called at any time.

◆ onPlaybackShutdown()

void org.schabi.newpipe.player.playback.PlaybackListener.onPlaybackShutdown ( )

Called when the play queue can no longer to played or used. Currently, this means the play queue is empty and complete. Signals to the listener that it should shutdown.

May be called at any time.
